Bioinformatics Resources

PDB to FASTA converter

 		Upload a PDB file and recieve FASTA output. Results will
be displayed on screen as well as emailed to user.

DNA to Protein translator

 		Paste in a valid DNA sequence and recieve the translated protein sequence
on screen for your choice of any or all of the reading frames

Gene Viewer (Cow)

 		Choose a location on any of the chromosomes and recieve on screen info
of all the genes in that region as well as a graphical display
